# ☕ 9Coffee — Social Media Marketing Plan

> Group 672 | Digital Marketing Project

A complete social media marketing strategy built for **9Coffee**, 
a specialty coffee café located in October City, Giza — 
owned and marketed by our team.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

To provide every customer with a premium, personalized coffee 
experience by offering the widest variety of coffee drinks at 
accessible prices, in a warm and welcoming environment in October City.

---

## 🌟 Our Vision

To become the #1 go-to specialty coffee destination in October City 
and expand 9Coffee's presence as a recognized local brand across 
Greater Cairo, known for quality, consistency, and community.
